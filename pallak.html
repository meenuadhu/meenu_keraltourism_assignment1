Palakkad





Well, not all the tourist destinations in Kerala is all about scenic coastlines; Palakkad is just one of them! Though this city is deprived of any coastal line, it has something distinctive that makes it a must visit place! Palakkad is the ‘Gateway to Kerala’; it connects the India’s Spice Captial with other parts of the country. Also, while you can find most of Kerala being dotted with coconut groves, Palakkad offers an overall contrasting view! While in this city, you will find yourself amidst never-ending Palmyra groves. Abundance of this giant trees with a crown at the top, got this city the name ‘Land of Palmyra Trees’. Be it Kerala’s history, education, literature, and cultural values, Palakkad has a remarkable contribution in each of them. Even if looked through agricultural aspects, this pictorial city plays a noteworthy role, and thus, it is also esteemed as a major granary to the state!
